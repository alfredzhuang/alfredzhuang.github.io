Background

While I learned a little bit of the basics of Java when I was younger, I didn't really get started on properly learning the coding language until senior year of high school. That year, I was given the opportunity to take AP Computer Science A, where we learned the fundamentals of Java. After enjoying my experiences coding in that course, I decided to major in Computer Science. In freshman year of University, I brushed up on my fundamentals of Java. Then, in my sophomore year of university, I began to branch out, trying out mobile development with flutter and beginning to learn full stack web development. Now, in my junior year of University, I'm learning more about web development and am excited to be able to experience the world of software engineering this summer as a Software Engineer Intern at Capital One.
