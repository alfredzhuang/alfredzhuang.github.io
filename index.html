After enjoying my experiences coding in that course, I decided to major in Computer Science. In freshman year of university, I brushed up on my fundamentals of Java. Experience

Capital One - Software Engineer Intern (June 2022 - August 2022)

I joined Capital One as a Software Engineer Intern under the 10 week Technology Internship Program in the summer of 2022. I got to work on a team of over 10 people to help build a full-stack application. In specific, I worked on the frontend portion of the application, focusing on using a tech stack that consisted of React, TypeScript, Jest, and Redux to build out the user interface. I also got the opportunity to integrate our new project with another already existing project where I primarily used React, TypeScript, and Jest to build out the UI. Through that experience, I also got to learn more about GraphQL as it was a part of the implementation that I needed to work with to render information in the UI. In addition to working on this application throughout the duration of the summer, I got to participate in Capital One Coders (a program that focuses on building the future technologists in local communities), where I mentored students that were learning HTML and CSS. I also was able to get the AWS Certified Practitioner Certificate, which I studied and tested for during the internship. I was also able to attend 2 in-person events that were organized by Capital One, where I got to meet fellow interns and associates at Capital One. The first event was held in the office that I was based out of (San Francisco, CA) and the other was held in the headquarters (McLean, Virginia).
